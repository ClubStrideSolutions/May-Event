Event Overview

Career Exploration Student-Led Live Streamed Networking

This collaborative event showcases innovative student-led health initiatives while providing valuable career exploration opportunities for youth interested in public health fields. Experience the powerful integration of digital marketing, data analysis, and community outreach in addressing critical health priorities.

About the Organizers

The Social Impact Program is a student-led initiative in Vallejo High Schools. Through innovative advocacy, SIP addresses community health with three specialized teams focused on peer-to-peer education, digital advocacy, and assessing data-driven impact.
